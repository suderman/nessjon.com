Our Story

Welcome to our wedding website! We look forward to celebrating our special day with you.

Schedule of Events for July 6, 2013

2 pm: Wedding ceremony begins. This formal service is held outdoors in the Zoo Gardens north of the Enmax Conservatory. Please arrive 15-20 minutes prior. If it is raining, the ceremony will be held inside the Safari Lodge.

3-5 pm: Zoo-time! While the wedding party has photos taken, the guests have two hours to enjoy the zoo.

5 pm: Dinner reception begins at the Safari Lodge. Dance to follow.

Parking

Use the July 6 Zoo Pass you received with your invitation. Each pass allows one person to enter the zoo free of charge. Please keep these with you as you may be asked to show them at the reception. Please note that pets are not permitted entry at the Calgary Zoo.

Zoo Entry

North Lot:
The main zoo parking lot and entrance is located off St. George’s Drive, just north of Memorial Drive with zoo access through the north gate. A $7.00 daily parking fee is in effect.

Dress Considerations

The wedding is formal, and we invite you to dress in style! Zoo-themed clothing welcomed! However, as this is an outdoor wedding, we do encourage guests to bring comfortable footwear for walking, and to bring extra clothing in case of rain.

Zoo Details and Maps

For a map of the Calgary zoo, directions, and any other zoo-related inquiries you may have, please visit the Calgary Zoo website at http://www.calgaryzoo.ca/

Accommodations

Guests are responsible for arranging their own accommodations and transportation. We encourage making these arrangements early.
